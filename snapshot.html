FAQ

How long does delivery take?
SLA is 24 hours. In practice most reports ship in under 10 minutes because the scanner runs in 2.2 seconds and the PDF renders in 2-3 seconds.
Is this a penetration test?
No. Passive external audit only. We do not attempt to exploit anything, do not authenticate against your site, do not extract data. The scanner behaves like an anonymous visitor.
What if I need help fixing what you find?
The PDF maps every finding to either a free control your developer can configure or a Pejji remediation package (from ₦60,000). No pressure, hand the report to anyone.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, unconditional within 7 days of delivery. Email hello@securva.net. If the scanner fails and you never got a PDF, refund is automatic.
Is my domain data stored?
Your email + URL stored 90 days then auto-deleted. The PDF is on a signed URL that expires in 14 days. We do not store site content beyond the audit itself.
