FAQ

The ten questions we get most often.

What exactly is a Securva Snapshot?
A self-serve PDF report on your website's security posture and NDPA 2023 compliance. You submit a URL, our scanner runs 42 security checkpoints and 18 NDPA requirements against it, and we deliver a 12-20 page PDF with findings ordered by severity and mapped to fixes. Automated end-to-end, no human in the delivery loop.
How long does delivery take?
Our SLA is 24 hours. In practice most reports ship within 5 minutes of URL submission because the scanner runs in 2.2 seconds and the PDF renders in 2-3 seconds. The 24-hour buffer is for days when the scanner queue is backed up or a domain needs a human sanity check.
What happens if my site scores badly?
You get a list of every issue, ordered by severity, with a specific fix for each. Most fixes are one-line configuration changes. You can hand the report to your existing developer, or hire Pejji (our sister agency) to rebuild your site from the Card tier at ₦60,000. Or just sit with the report for a month and decide. No pressure.
Is this a penetration test?
No. A Snapshot is a passive external audit. We do not attempt to exploit anything, we do not authenticate against your site, we do not try to extract data. The scanner behaves like an anonymous visitor. A real penetration test is a different product class, we can refer you to partners who do that if you need it.
Can I share the PDF with my team?
Yes. You own the PDF once delivered. Share internally, send to your developer, file it with your DPIA documentation, whatever you need. The Pro tier adds a 90-day re-scan so you can track remediation progress across quarters.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, unconditional within 7 days of delivery. Email hello@securva.net and we refund the Gumroad charge within 24 hours. If the scanner failed and you never got a PDF, the refund is automatic.
What languages do you support?
The PDF is in English with Nigerian context (naira pricing, NDPC regulatory references, local examples). For non-technical readers we also support pidgin explanations on request. Add a note when you submit your URL.
Is my domain data stored?
Your email + submitted URL are stored for 90 days then automatically deleted. The scan output (PDF + scan JSON) is stored behind a signed URL that expires in 14 days. We do not store any content fetched from your site beyond the audit itself. Full data handling details in our privacy policy.
How does this compare to an enterprise audit?
An enterprise audit is typically $5,000 to $15,000, takes 4-8 weeks, and produces 100+ pages. Suitable for listed companies or regulated fintechs facing formal audits. The Snapshot is intentionally simpler, cheaper, and faster: self-serve, $29, 24 hours, 12-20 pages, focused on the 80% of controls that matter most for Nigerian SMEs. Use the Snapshot first. If your findings warrant deeper work, escalate.
Can I use Paystack instead of Gumroad?
Yes, for Nigerian customers who prefer naira payments via Paystack, email hello@securva.net and we send a payment link with the same fulfillment flow. The Gumroad buttons above are the default for international (USD) customers.
